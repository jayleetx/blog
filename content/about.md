Hi! I'm a research fellow with the [Early Voting Information Center](https://blogs.reed.edu/earlyvoting/) at [Reed College](https://www.reed.edu/) in Portland, OR. EVIC does research into election science, early voting and vote-by-mail, and public opinion, but I'm also interested in statistics and data science. While a student at Reed, I wrote my [senior thesis](https://github.com/jayleetx/thesis/blob/master/index/_book/thesis.pdf) on some of the demographic and electoral impacts of ranked choice voting on voter turnout in San Francisco. Aside from all that, I like to make music, go hiking, and bike around Portland.
